Gxated - The best survival multiplayer server

What is Gxated SMP? Gxated SMP is a Minecraft survival multiplayer server. We are trying to build a community similar to the Hermitcraft server. If you want to join in, connect to 

                             gxatedsmp.minehut.gg

Our rules are:

                   1. No griefing. No destroying other peoples builds, pretty self explanatory.

                   2. No cheating. No use of hacked client or other modifications that give an unfair advantage. Schematica is allowed though.

                   3.  Don't be toxic. Be nice to others. No one likes it when somebody joins and puts everybody in a bad mood.

                   (These rules do not apply to member bruo)
